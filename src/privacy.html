Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Overview

Christ Church Anglican Bluffton, Inc. ("Christ Church Bluffton," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use this website or make a donation.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information you voluntarily provide, including your name, email address, mailing address, and payment information when you make a donation. We also collect standard web analytics data such as browser type, pages visited, and referring URLs.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to process donations, provide tax-deductible contribution receipts, communicate with you about church activities, and improve our website. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Donation Processing

Online donations are processed through Tithe.ly, a third-party payment processor. We do not store your credit card or bank account information on our servers. Please refer to Tithe.ly's privacy policy for information on how they handle your payment data.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Cookies

This website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.
