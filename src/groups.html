Life Together

Find Your Community

Faith was never meant to be lived alone. At Christ Church Bluffton, we believe that spiritual growth happens best in the context of authentic community. Whether gathered around a dinner table, joined in prayer, or studying Scripture together, our groups are where real life and real faith intersect.

Bible Study

Table 246

"And day by day, attending the temple together and breaking bread in their homes, they received their food with glad and generous hearts." — Acts 2:46

Table 246 is the heartbeat of our community life. Inspired by the early church in Acts 2:46, these home-based Bible studies are where we gather around tables, open God's Word, and share life together. Each group is led by a trained host who guides the conversation and creates a welcoming space for everyone — whether you have been walking with Jesus for decades or are just beginning to explore faith.

We currently have 5 hosts throughout the Bluffton area, each opening their home weekly to foster deeper relationships and spiritual growth. Tables are intentionally kept small so that every voice is heard and every person is known.

Whether you want to join an existing table or open your home to host one, there is a place for you.

Prayer Groups

Prayer is not just something we do — it is who we are. At Christ Church Bluffton, we believe that praying together transforms both our hearts and our community.

Our prayer groups meet regularly to lift up the needs of our church family, our neighborhood, and the world. These gatherings are a sacred space where we bring our burdens, our thanksgivings, and our hopes before the Lord together. Whether you are experienced in intercessory prayer or simply want to learn, you are welcome here.

We also maintain an active prayer chain so that urgent needs can be lifted up by the community throughout the week. No request is too small, and no burden is too heavy for us to carry together in prayer.
