Who We Are

About Christ Church

Our Mission

Christ Church Bluffton is a new Anglican church plant coming to Bluffton, South Carolina in 2026. We are a community of believers united by a shared love for Christ and a desire to see His Kingdom grow in the Lowcountry.

We are reformed and catholic Christians, rooted in the historic faith passed down through the centuries. We hold firmly to the authority of Scripture, the ancient creeds, the sacraments, and the historic episcopate as the pillars of our faith and practice.

Verify

Christ Church Bluffton is a member of the Anglican Church in North America (ACNA), a province of the global Anglican Communion committed to the authority of Scripture, the centrality of the Gospel, and the power of the Holy Spirit.

Our Vision

What Drives Us

Gospel-Centered

Everything we do is rooted in the truth of Scripture and the good news of Jesus Christ. The Gospel shapes our worship, our community, and our mission to the world.

Ancient Worship

We worship using the Book of Common Prayer, drawing from centuries of Christian liturgy. Our services connect us to the ancient faith while speaking to the present day.

Community Focused

We believe in common grace, common prayer, and common life for the common good. Our church exists not just for ourselves, but to serve and bless the Bluffton community.

What We Believe

Our Beliefs

As Anglicans, we stand within a rich spiritual heritage that stretches back through the centuries to the early Church. Our faith is built upon four essential pillars that have guided Christians throughout the ages.

01

Scripture

The Holy Scriptures of the Old and New Testaments are the Word of God written, containing all things necessary for salvation, and serving as the ultimate rule and standard of faith.

02

Creeds

The Apostles' Creed and the Nicene Creed serve as sufficient statements of the Christian faith, summarizing the essential doctrines believed by Christians throughout history.

03

Sacraments

The two sacraments ordained by Christ himself -- Baptism and the Supper of the Lord -- are administered with unfailing use of His words of institution and the elements ordained by Him.

04

Historic Episcopate

The historic episcopate, locally adapted, provides the continuity of apostolic leadership and governance that has been a hallmark of the Church since its earliest days.

Meet Our Pastor

Rev. Jonathan Riddle

Lead Pastor & Church Planter

Rev. Jonathan Riddle is the founding pastor and church planter of Christ Church Bluffton. Called to ministry with a deep love for Anglican worship and theology, he brings a passion for building Christ-centered communities rooted in the historic faith.

With a heart for the Lowcountry, Pastor Riddle is committed to establishing a church that serves as a beacon of hope, grace, and truth in Bluffton. He believes that the Anglican tradition offers a unique and beautiful expression of the Christian faith that speaks to people from all walks of life.

Pastor Riddle's vision for Christ Church is to create a welcoming community where people can encounter the living God through Word and Sacrament, grow in their faith through discipleship, and go out to serve their neighbors and the world.
