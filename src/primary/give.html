Partner With Us

Generosity is at the heart of the Christian life. When we give, we reflect the boundless generosity of God, who gave His only Son for the life of the world.

Your gifts enable Christ Church Bluffton to grow from a vision into a vibrant, Christ-centered community in the Lowcountry. Every contribution — large or small — directly supports our mission to bring the historic Anglican faith to Bluffton.

Your Gift Helps Us

  • ✓ Secure a worship space for our growing congregation
  • ✓ Develop ministries that nurture faith and discipleship
  • ✓ Create welcoming environments for families and newcomers
  • ✓ Serve our neighbors through outreach and compassion
  • ✓ Equip leaders to shepherd and teach with excellence

“Each one must give as he has decided in his heart, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver.”

— 2 Corinthians 9:7 (ESV)
