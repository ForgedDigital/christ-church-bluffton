Our Mission

Rooted in Tradition,
Growing in Grace

Christ Church Bluffton is an Anglican church plant launching in 2026. We are committed to the historic Christian faith as expressed through the Anglican tradition — a faith both catholic and reformed, ancient and alive.

We believe the Church is called to be a faithful presence in the world, extending Christ's love through Word and Sacrament, prayer and service.
